    ABOUT THE BOOKSTORE

    A BOOK A DAY KEEPS THE BOREDOM AWAY

    Welcome to the Ninh's bookstore online, the place where you can find books for every taste and interest.

    This bookstore is owned by Ninh Pho, a really enthusiastic book lover who has a passion for sharing his knowledge and recommendations with others. Whether you are looking for fiction, non-fiction, classics, or new releases, you will find something to suit your needs and preferences here. You can browse through our categories, search by keywords, or ask for suggestions from our friendly staff. You can also join our online community of readers and writers, where you can exchange opinions, reviews, and tips.

    We hope you enjoy your visit and come back often!

    BEST SELLERS OF FICTION

    The Seven Husbands of Evelyn Hugo

    by Taylor Jenkins Reid

    "A spellbinding novel about love, glamour and the price of fame." - Publishers Weekly

    "A mesmerizing journey through the splendor of old Hollywood into the harsh realities of the present day." - Kirkus Reviews

    They Both Die At The End

    by Adam Silvera

    "Silvera once again conveys strong messages about love, loss, grief, acceptance, and joy in his work." - Common Sense Media

    "A moving and thought-provoking novel that reminds us to live every day as if it's our last." - Kirkus Reviews

    If Cats Disappeared from the World

    by Genki Kawamura

    "A charming, quirky and life-affirming tale that proves kindness really does make all the difference." - The Guardian

    "A charming and bittersweet tale of what really matters in life." - Publishers Weekly

    BEST SELLERS OF NON-FICTION

    Outlive

    by Bill Gifford and Peter Attia

    "We are stuck in an earlier era of healthcare protocols and health-related habits. A major reset is needed. Life spans may improve thereby." - The Wall Street Journal

    Atomic Habits

    by James Clear

    "While I can't say that the information in it is that different from other books I've read on the topic, it's definitely the best breakdown of said info that I've seen." - Books with Emily Fox

    Spare

    by Prince Harry, Duke of Sussex

    “At once emotional and embittered, the royal memoir is mired in a paradox: drawing endless attention in an effort to renounce fame.” - The New York Times

    “By turns sympathetic and absurd, this is a memoir that deals in the tropes of tabloid storytelling even as it lambasts them.” - The Guardian
